Sienar is a revival of an obscure display typeface known as Chariot.

Chariot appeared most prominently in a little film called Star Wars, where it was nearly illegible, before fading into total obscurity...

Until now.

Chariot is reborn as Sienar—named for the fictional manufacturer of the TIE Fighters whose consoles Chariot once illuminated.

The typeface now comprises two fonts: Sienar AF which matches the way it was implemented in Star Wars, as an alien language, while Sienar AF Chariot is a more faithful recreation of the original typeface, with support for extended latin characters.
