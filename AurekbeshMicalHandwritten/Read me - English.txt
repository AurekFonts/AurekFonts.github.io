Aurekbesh Mical Handwritten
---------------------------------------------------------------
Index:
---------------------------------------------------------------
Contains the pupular script "Aurekbesh" from Star Wars written
by Mical from "Star Wars- Knights of the old Republic 2". 
---------------------------------------------------------------
Installation:
---------------------------------------------------------------
Copy the Ttf data file "Aurekbesh Mical Handwritten" in your 
"Font" folder, where your other sricps should be, too. You'll 
find it certainly at: "C:/WINDOWS/Fonts" than insert it and
you've got Aurekbesh written by Mical on your computer! ^^
---------------------------------------------------------------
Uninstallation:
---------------------------------------------------------------
Just delete at "C:/WINDOWS/Fonts" the Ttf data file "Aurekbesh
Mical Handwritten".
---------------------------------------------------------------
Use:
---------------------------------------------------------------

There some problems at creating a handwritten script of
Aurekbesh, because there're some letters which don't exist in
english or german. That's why I correlated this signs with
otherones. What sort of that you can see here: Go on your
Desktop on "Start/Programms/Supplies/Systemprogramm/
Charactermap" and chose ar "Script" "Aurekbesh Mical
Handwritten". ^^
