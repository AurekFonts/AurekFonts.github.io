Aurekbesh
---------------------------------------------------------------
Index:
---------------------------------------------------------------
Contains the pupular script "Aurekbesh" from Star Wars.
---------------------------------------------------------------
Installation:
---------------------------------------------------------------
Copy the Pfm data file "Aurekbesh" in your "Font" folder, where 
your other sricps should be, too. You'll find it certainly ar: 
"C:/WINDOWS/Fonts" than insert it and you've got Aurekbesh on 
your computer! ^^
---------------------------------------------------------------
Uninstallation:
---------------------------------------------------------------
Just delete at "C:/WINDOWS/Fonts" the Pfm data file "Aurekbesh".
---------------------------------------------------------------
Use:
---------------------------------------------------------------
I had some problems by working on that script. 'Cause I couldn't 
allot some Letters to a key, for example "Orenth", what "oo" 
means. So I had to name the letters diffrent. So here's a 
direction for all of the letters I couldn't allot: 
(You can look at the key combination at "Start/ Programm/ 
Accessoiries/ System Programm/ Sign Table" and choose 
"Aurekbesh".)

You've to tipe combinations of keys now, there's standing by 
for example:

Cherk (Ch)= Ç/ç (Alt+0199/Alt+0231)

That means, that the letter Cherk correspond with C cedile (Ç). 
For that you've to push the Alt key while typing the numbers 
at the right side of your keyboard, here: 0199

Understood? Good! :3
So we can go on! :D

Cherk (Ch)= Ç/ç (Alt+0199/ Alt+0231)
Enth (Ae)= Æ/æ (Alt+0198/ Alt+0230), or at a german keyboard simply "Ä/ä"
Onith (Oe)= Œ/œ (Alt+0140/ Alt+0156), or at a german keyboard simply "Ö/ö"
Krenth (Kh)= Ð/ð (Eth like krEnTH) (Alt+208/ Alt+0240)
Nen (Ng)= Ñ/ñ (Alt+0209/ Alt+0241)
Orentth (Oo)= Ó/ó (Alt+0211/ Alt+0243)
Shen (Sh)= É/é (Alt+0201/ Alt+0233)
Thesh (th)= þ/Þ (Thorn like THesh) (Alt+0254/ Alt+0222)
'links= ´ Akut-Akzent (Alt+0180), oder "´"
'rechts= `/¸ Cedille (Alt+0184)
"Anführungszeichen Links= """
"Anführungszeichen rechts= "#"

I hope these are all! ^^;
